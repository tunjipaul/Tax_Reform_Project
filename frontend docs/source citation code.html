Could you clarify the new adjustments to the VAT threshold for small businesses in the 2024 reform bill? Specifically regarding the exemption limit.

smart_toy
Tax Assistant AI

Based on the 2024 reforms, the Value Added Tax (VAT) threshold has been significantly adjusted to reduce the compliance burden on Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).

Specifically, businesses with an annual turnover of less than N25 million are now fully exempt from charging and remitting VAT. This adjustment aims to stimulate growth in the small business sector by lowering administrative costs.
